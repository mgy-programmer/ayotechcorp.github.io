Frequently Asked Questions

Question 1: How Can I Become a Travel Guide?

Click on the hamburger menu and select 'Become a Travel Guide.' In the ensuing window, fill in your details and submit the application. Following the application, provide two documents for approval: one to confirm the correctness of your specified IBAN number and another to validate the accuracy of the provided information. Upon review and approval, you will officially become a travel guide.

Question 2: Do I need a document to become a travel guide?

To become a travel guide, ensure you meet the legal requirements in your country. If not, AyoTravel won't accept any responsibility. You are solely responsible for paying taxes on your earnings and obtaining the necessary legal documents.

Question 3: After becoming a travel guide, is it possible to switch my guide status to passive?

Yes, you can. Navigate to your profile, click on the three dots at the top left, and choose 'Edit My Profile.' Once on the Edit Profile page, you can deactivate your travel guide status.
